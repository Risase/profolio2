About Me

Risase Okemwa: Web Designer & Front-End Developer

 Hi there! I'm Risase Okemwa, a passionate web designer and front-end developer with a knack for combining aesthetics with functionality. With a background in both creative design and technical development, I specialize in building user-centric websites that not only look great but are also easy to navigate. My goal is to transform complex ideas into engaging digital experiences that captivate users and make information accessible.

Background


My journey into web development started in college, where I initially majored in graphic design. While I loved the artistic side of design, I found myself drawn to the technical challenges of bringing designs to life on the web. I switched my focus to web design and front-end development, blending my visual skills with the technical expertise needed to create interactive, responsive websites. Since then, I’ve been dedicated to mastering the intersection of design and technology.
